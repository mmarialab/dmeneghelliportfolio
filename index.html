Lighting Design

Daniela is a graduated architect master in lighting design with seven years experience in this field. Her interest in lighting started as an architecture student in Brazil, where daylight was always a guideline to her projects. Working for the most established lighting practices in London and Rio de Janeiro, she has delivered prestigious projects worldwide. With her diligent work and ability to coordinate projects of different types and scales, Daniela became a skilled lighting designer with expertise in high end residential, hospitality, art collection, and commercial projects. Her traveller and creative nature combined with sensibility and technical knowledge reveal the ability to make every project unique, bringing the magic of light no matter where.
